Data Analysis on Gold Zone Players Club Membership

The analysis of the Gold Zone Players Club membership data provides valuable insights into the spending patterns, game preferences, and demographics of the club's members.

Dataset Overview

  • memberID: Unique identifier for each member.
  • age_start: Age of the member when they joined.
  • dist_park: Distance to the park in miles.
  • gender: Gender of the member (Male/Female).
  • job_type: Job type of the member (Salaried, Hourly, Unknown).

Key Insights from the Data

Game Popularity Trends

"Star Wars" is the most popular game, followed by "Connect 4 Hoops" and "Wheel Deal".

Spending by Job Type

Salaried members typically spend more, but high-earning hourly workers also exhibit strong spending capacity.

Conclusion

  • Game Popularity: "Star Wars" is the most popular, while "Dance Dance Revolution" attracts high spenders.
  • Spending Behavior: Most members spend between $4,000 and $6,000.
  • Demographic Insights: Game preferences vary by gender and age.
