Analyzing Campaign Impact on Customer Spending at Lobster Land

This analysis explores how different marketing campaigns influenced customer behavior and spending patterns at Lobster Land.

Understanding the Data

  • customerID: Unique identifier for each customer.
  • campaign_seen: The campaign a customer was exposed to.
  • total_spending: The amount of money spent by the customer.

Campaign Distribution

The Family Fun Campaign had the highest number of participants, followed by the Relaxation and Leisure Drive.

Analyzing Customer Spending

The Loyalty Reward Push campaign led to the highest mean spending, suggesting loyalty programs effectively drive higher customer spending.

Statistical Testing for Campaign Effectiveness

Two-sample t-tests were conducted to determine significant differences in spending across campaigns.

Chi-Square Goodness of Fit Test

A chi-square test showed that customer preferences for attractions followed historical trends.

Key Insights and Recommendations

  • Loyalty Programs: Returning customers spend more; tailored incentives can further boost revenue.
  • Targeted Marketing: Campaigns tailored for thrill-seekers and relaxation-seekers can enhance engagement.
  • Family Packages: Bundled deals for families can maximize participation and spending.

Conclusion

By analyzing customer spending and campaign exposure data, Lobster Land can refine marketing strategies to attract diverse visitors while maximizing revenue.
